OUR PROGRAMS

Founded on the belief that strong communities are built through collective action, we run community-driven initiatives to empower lives and create lasting change. We work tirelessly to create opportunities for growth, education and positive social impact.
We run programs that address the most pressing needs of communities in Migori County and beyond.
Our programs are designed to improve livelihoods, protect the environment, and empower vulnerable groups to live with dignity and resilience.
