OUR PROGRAMS

Founded on the belief that strong communities are built through collective action, we run community-driven initiatives to empower lives and create lasting change. We work tirelessly to create opportunities for growth, education and positive social impact.
We run programs that address the most pressing needs of communities in Migori County and beyond.
Our programs are designed to improve livelihoods, protect the environment, and empower vulnerable groups to live with dignity and resilience.

Protect Human Rights

We advocate for the rights of individuals and communities affected by mining and business activities, ensuring fairness and justice.

Safe Mining

We conduct trainings to mining communities on importance of safe mining techniques and use of protective geers

Health & Wellness

Community health awareness, free medical camps, and access to clean water.

Community Empowerment

Through awareness campaigns, dialogue, and partnerships, we build stronger, more informed communities capable of driving positive change

Our Commitments

All our programs are community-driven,inclusive and sustainable. We put people first ensuring that every innitiativeis rooted in dignity, equality and transparency. By strengthening community capacity, we aim to create lasting changes for present and future generations.
