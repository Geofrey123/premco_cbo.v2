Our Projects

At Precious Metals Community Outreach (PreMCO), we implement impactful projects that improve livelihoods, protect the environment, and empower communities across Migori County and beyond.

Ongoing Income Generating Activities (IGAs)

Poultry Farming

We provide women groups with improved kienyeji chicken, veterinary support, and equipment to strengthen household income and improve food security year-round.

Tree Nursery

Through community tree nurseries, members earn extra income while contributing to reforestation, climate resilience, and sustainable environmental management.

Goat Rearing

We support households with goats as seed capital to establish sustainable livestock enterprises, ensuring long-term financial stability and improved nutrition.

Environmental & Community Projects

Water Safety & Accessibility

We work to improve clean water access through underground tanks, dams, and rehabilitation of natural water sources, reducing pollution from mining and protecting health.

Food Security Support

We train small-scale farmers, provide certified seeds, and promote sustainable farming practices to enhance household food production and resilience.

Conservation & Climate Action

We promote tree planting, forest conservation, cage fishing, and eco-tourism to safeguard natural resources while creating local green enterprises.

Future Plans

  • Supporting miners with the right equipments and protective gears
  • Construction of a Community Resource Center to equip youth with career guidance, skills, and opportunities.
  • Establishment of a Voluntary Counselling and Testing (VCT) center to support youth and women's health.
  • Expansion of conservation-based enterprises including agroforestry and eco-tourism initiatives.
  • Scaling up IGAs to reach more households across Migori and neighboring counties.
