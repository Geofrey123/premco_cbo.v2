About Us

Precious Metals Community Outreach (PREMCO) is a non-profit Community Based Organization dedicated to fostering sustainable development, economic empowerment, and social well-being within underserved mining communities. Our name, Precious Metals, symbolizes resilience, value, and long-term impact qualities that define our mission to uplift individuals and transform societies. At PREMCO, we believe that every individual has the potential to thrive when given access to the right resources, skills, and opportunities. Our work primarily focuses on underprivileged communities, artisanal miners, small-scale entrepreneurs, and marginalized groups, equipping them with the tools needed to achieve financial independence and improved livelihoods. Through our skills training, financial literacy, and social welfare programs, we empower individuals to break the cycle of poverty, enhance their economic prospects, and contribute to the overall well-being of their families and communities.

Our holistic approach ensures that communities not only gain economic stability but also develop the resilience needed to sustain long-term progress. By fostering partnerships with stakeholders, advocating for fair labour practices, and promoting sustainable economic solutions, PREMCO remains committed to creating meaningful opportunities for growth and self sufficiency. Our Goals

Our goal is to empower wetland and mining communities by improving livelihoods, protecting natural resources, and promoting sustainable development.

We have implemented several innovative projects aimed at addressing key challenges in local mining areas. Our initiatives focus on improving water accessibility, promoting environmental sustainability, and enhancing community livelihoods. We have supported small-scale miners by introducing safer working methods, waste management solutions, and capacity-building programs that encourage responsible mining practices. Through these efforts, we continue to foster growth, safety, and long-term development within the mining communities we serve.

KEY OBJECTIVES

    Workplace Safety:
    Advocate for and provide access to high-quality, gender-responsive Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
    and training to enhance the occupational health and safety of female and male workers in hazardous industries.
    Gender Equity:
    Address systemic challenges that disproportionately affect women in the workforce by promoting equal opportunities,
    rights, and safe working conditions.
    Capacity Building:
    Equip women and vulnerable groups with skills in occupational safety, financial literacy, and rights advocacy
    to foster self-reliance and informed decision-making.
    Community Collaboration:
    Partner with local authorities, industry stakeholders, and community leaders to implement sustainable safety and economic empowerment initiatives.
    Economic Empowerment:
    Enhance women's productivity and financial independence through training in savings, entrepreneurship,
    and access to Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLA).

SCOPE OF WORK

PREMCO main focus areas are on;


  1. Financial education is a cornerstone of economic independence,through Financial Literacy Village Savings and Loans (VSL) Training PreMCO provides;
    • Financial Literacy Training Educating individuals on savings, budgeting, responsible borrowing, and investment strategies.
    • Village Savings and Loans (VSL) Training Establishing community-led savings and credit groups to provide financial access
      and security for artisans and small entrepreneurs.
    • Entrepreneurship Support Helping small-scale business owners, especially women, access capital and business training to improve their enterprises.
    • Digital and Mobile Banking Education Introducing communities to digital financial services to enhance financial inclusion.

  2. Health & Safety Training providing education on occupational health hazards, proper use of protective equipment, and safe mining practices. Women working in artisanal and small-scale mining (ASM) often face health risks and unsafe working conditions.
    PreMCO addresses these challenges through:

    • Access to Protective Gear & Equipment Distributing safety kits and promoting the use of safe tools and materials.
    • Environmental Awareness & Sustainable Practices Encouraging eco-friendly mining techniques to reduce environmental and health risks.
    • Advocacy for Safe Working Conditions Partnering with stakeholders to implement policies that protect women and marginalized workers.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Our programs primarily target:


  • Small-scale miners and their families.
  • Local community members in mining regions.
  • Local governments and policymakers in mining areas.
  • Youth and women interested in economic empowerment and sustainable mining practices.
  • Non-governmental organizations (NGOs) and development partners focused on mining communities.

WORKING MODALITIES

PREMCO adopts a participatory approach in all its programs, working closely with local communities to understand their needs and collaboratively design interventions.

The organization uses:


  • Workshops and training sessions.
  • Public awareness campaigns and community outreach programs.
  • Collaboration with local authorities, stakeholders, and mining companies.
  • Monitoring and evaluation to assess the impact of interventions.
