Who we are

PreMCO (Precious Metal Community Outreach) is a Community-Based Organization (CBO) dedicated to promoting safe mining practices, responsible business conduct, and the protection of human rights in our communities. We believe that sustainable development can only be achieved when economic growth goes hand in hand with respect for people and the environment.

Our Mission

To protect and promote safe mining, business, and human rights by empowering communities with knowledge, advocacy, and support for ethical and sustainable practices.

We believe that every community has the strength and wisdom to overcomechallenges when given the right support and resources. Our role is to facilitate, empower, and walk alongside communities on their journey towards self-sufficiency and prosperity.

Our vision

A society where mining and business activities are conducted safely, responsibly, and in full respect of human dignity and environmental sustainability.

Economic Empowerment

We provide financial literacy, VSL training, and support small-scale entrepreneurs.

Safe Mining

We equip youths and women miners with PPE and train them on occupational health & safety.

Environment

We conduct campaigns and trainings on tree planting, water protection, and environment conservation initiatives for sustainable futures.

Join Us in Transforming Lives

Our goal is to empower wetland and mining communities by improving livelihoods, protecting natural resources, and promoting sustainable development. We work to reduce poverty, safeguard the environment, and create opportunities that enable families to live with dignity, resilience, and hope for a better future.
