Who we are

Precious Metals Community Outreach (PREMCO) is a non-profit Community Based Organization dedicated to fostering sustainable development, economic empowerment, and social well-being within underserved communities. Our name, PREMCO, symbolizes resilience, value, and long-term impact qualities that define our mission to uplift individuals and transform societies.

Our Mission

To empower underserved communities by providing access to essential resources, educational opportunities, and sustainable development programs that foster economic growth, social inclusion, and environmental stewardship.

We believe that every community has the strength and wisdom to overcomechallenges when given the right support and resources. Our role is to facilitate, empower, and walk alongside communities on their journey towards self-sufficiency and prosperity.

Join Us in Transforming Lives

Our goal at Precious Metals Community Outreach (PreMCO) is to empower wetland and mining communities by improving livelihoods, protecting natural resources, and promoting sustainable development. We work to reduce poverty, safeguard the environment, and create opportunities that enable families to live with dignity, resilience, and hope for a better future.
